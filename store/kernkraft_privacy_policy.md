# Kernkraft Consulting Inc. — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

---

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications and services published by Kernkraft Consulting Inc., including **Nuclear Message of the Day** ("Nuclear MOTD"), **Nuclear Quiz**, and **Sports Rostering** (collectively, "our Apps", "we", "us", or "our"). It explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when creating an account or using our Apps:

**All Apps:**
- **Account Information:** Name and email address
- **Optional Profile Data:** Phone number and profile photo, collected only if you choose to provide them
- **Push Notification Token:** A device token used to deliver push notifications, if you grant permission
- **Usage Data:** Basic information about how you interact with our Apps, used to improve the service

**Nuclear MOTD only:**
- Company or organization (optional)
- Country and timezone
- Selected topic preferences and email delivery preferences
- Safety insights or content you voluntarily submit for publication

**Sports Rostering only:**
- Team memberships, event RSVPs, availability responses, and drop-in sign-ups you submit
- Player position preferences (per team)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide the core features of each App (see app-specific detail below)
- Deliver push notifications you have opted into
- Respond to your support requests
- Send administrative information and service updates
- Improve and optimize our Apps
- Comply with legal obligations

**Nuclear MOTD:** Deliver personalized daily messages based on your topic preferences; send scheduled email notifications at your preferred time.

**Sports Rostering:** Display your name to members of teams you belong to; show your RSVP responses to team admins; coordinate drop-in session sign-ups.

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- With your consent
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights and safety
- With service providers who assist in operating our Apps (Firebase / Google Cloud), under strict confidentiality agreements

**Sports Rostering:** Your name is visible to other members of teams you join. Your availability responses are visible to team admins. Drop-in sign-up status is visible to team members for sessions you join.

We do not share personal data with advertisers beyond what is required for ad serving (see Advertising section).

---

## 4. Coach Rankings — Private Data (Sports Rostering)

Player rankings entered by team admins are strictly private. Players cannot view their own rankings or those of other players. Rankings are used only internally to assist with lineup generation and are never exposed to players or third parties.

---

## 5. Data Storage & Region

All data is stored in Google Cloud / Firebase infrastructure:

- **Nuclear MOTD:** Primary data stored in Google Cloud (us-central region); email delivery via AWS SES
- **Sports Rostering:** Data stored in Google Cloud Firestore, northamerica-northeast2 region (Toronto, Canada), selected for PIPEDA compliance

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services.

- **Nuclear MOTD:** You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at privacy@nuclear-motd.com
- **Sports Rostering:** You may permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the App (Profile → Delete Account). Deletion cascades through all app data and cannot be undone.

---

## 7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

- Encryption of data in transit (TLS) and at rest
- Firebase Security Rules that enforce per-user access controls
- No logging of personal data in crash reporting tools

---

## 8. Advertising

The free version of **Sports Rostering** displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers and usage data to show relevant ads in accordance with Google's privacy policy. A one-time "Remove Ads" purchase is available to permanently disable advertising.

**Nuclear MOTD** does not currently display third-party advertising.

---

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your data
- **Portability:** Request transfer of your data
- **Objection:** Object to certain processing of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at **privacy@nuclear-motd.com**

---

## 10. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our lawful bases for processing include consent, legitimate interests, and contractual necessity.

---

## 11. PIPEDA Compliance

For users in Canada, we comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). We obtain meaningful consent for the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

---

## 12. Push Notifications

Push notifications are optional and require your explicit permission. You may revoke notification permission at any time through your device settings. We will request permission with a clear explanation of what notifications will be sent before the system prompt appears.

---

## 13. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---

## 14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. For significant changes, we will provide notice through the App or by email where applicable.
