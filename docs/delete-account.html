Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Sport Rosters account at any time.

Option 1 — Delete In-App (Instant)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly inside the app:

  1. Open Sport Rosters and sign in.
  2. Tap the Profile icon (bottom navigation bar).
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All of your data — including your profile, team memberships, availability records, lineup assignments, and drop-in history — will be immediately and permanently deleted.

You will be signed out automatically once deletion is complete.

Option 2 — Request via Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

admin@nuclear-motd.com

Please include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 5 business days.

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile (name, email, photo, weight)
  • Your availability responses for all events
  • Your player rankings (coach-only records)
  • Your lineup assignments
  • Your drop-in session sign-ups
  • Your Firebase Authentication account

Team records (schedules, events, announcements) created by coaches are retained for other team members, but your personal data is removed from them.
